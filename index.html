About Us

Welcome to Chopstix Chinese And Momos, your culinary gateway to the vibrant flavors of China and Nepal right here in Dudulgaon, Pune. Our chefs craft each dish with authentic recipes and the freshest ingredients, promising an unforgettable dining experience. From sizzling noodles and aromatic curries to perfectly steamed momos, we invite you to embark on a delightful gastronomic journey with us.
